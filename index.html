About Our School

Nurturing Excellence and Fostering Leaders

Pinecrest Academy has been a beacon of academic excellence and character building for over two decades. We emphasize a comprehensive, student-centered approach to learning, preparing our pupils to thrive in a dynamically changing global society.

Our state-of-the-art campus offers a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to think critically, communicate effectively, and collaborate creatively. Through our rigorous academic curriculum and diverse co-curricular programs, we foster well-rounded individuals.

  • Modern STEAM-based Curriculum
  • Highly Qualified & Passionate Educators
  • State-of-the-art Science & Computer Labs
  • Vibrant Sports, Arts & Co-curricular Programs

Educational Programs We Offer

Providing a comprehensive pathway of learning from early childhood through advanced secondary studies.

Early Years (Nursery - UKG)

A playful and sensory learning experience designed to build early cognitive and social skills.

Primary School (Class 1 - 5)

Focusing on core literacy, numeracy, and scientific inquiry through interactive learning methodologies.

Middle School (Class 6 - 8)

Strengthening critical thinking, language capabilities, and problem-solving abilities across structured disciplines.

Secondary School (Class 9 - 10)

Preparing students for CBSE board examinations with balanced academic rigor and deep conceptual clarity.

Senior Secondary Commerce (Class 11 - 12)

Comprehensive courses in business studies, corporate accountancy, economics, and modern financial systems.
